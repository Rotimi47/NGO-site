About Us

At our charity, we are driven by a deep sense of compassion and a shared vision for a better world. With your generous support, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of those children in need. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to address pressing issues such as poverty, education, healthcare and environmental sustainability.
Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to empower individuals and communities, providing them with the tools and resources they need to thrive.

Our Mission

At WeCARE, we're on a mission to make a positive difference in the lives of children in Africa. Our goal is to address pressing social issues, such as poverty, education, and healthcare, by providing sustainable solutions and support to lacking communities. We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities and access to basic necessities. Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to tackle the root causes of poverty, inequality and social injustice in Africa. We focus on empowering marginalized communities, advocating for their rights, and providing them with the tools and resources they need to thrive. Our mission is not just about providiing immediate relief. It's about fostering long term change and empowering communities to become self-sufficient.

Our Vision

At iCARE, we envision a world where every individual is empowered to break free from the cycle of poverty and inequality. We dream of a society where access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities is not a privilege, but a fundamental right. Our vision is to create sustainable change by implementing innovative programs that address the root causes of social issues. Through advocacy, collaboration, and community engagement, we strive to build a future where everyone has equal opportunities to thrive and reach their full potential. Together, let's build a world where compassion and justice prevail, and no one is left behind. To make this vision a reality, we're working tirelessly on various fronts. We collaborate with local communities and governments to implement sustainable development practices.

How it started

I'm excited to share the inspiring story of how it all began for our charity. Picture this: a group of passionate individuals, just like you and me, came together with a burning desire to make a positive impact in the world. They were driven by personal experiences and a deep empathy for the struggles faced by marginalized communities. With hearts full of compassion and determination, they embarked on a journey to create change.
It started with small steps, like organizing local fundraisers and community events. These initial efforts brought people together, spreading awareness and raising much-needed funds. As the word spread and more individuals joined the cause, the momentum grew stronger. Volunteers poured in, offering their time, skills, and resources to support the mission.
But it wasn't just about the numbers. It was about the stories of transformation, the lives touched, and the hope ignited. Every success, no matter how small, fueled their passion and inspired them to dream bigger. With each milestone reached, they realized the power of collective action and the ripple effects it could create.
As the charity expanded, so did its reach. It started partnering with local organizations, governments, and international entities to maximize its impact.
